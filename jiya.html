Jiya Shankar will be participating in Bigg Boss OTT 2 as it is confirmed now. She is known for playing the characters of Iravati in Meri Hanikarak and Susheela in Kaatelal and Sons serial. Jiya has also hosted the show Good Night India on SAB TV. Recently she appeared in the Marathi film Ved with the best Ritesh Deshmukh and Genelia Deshmukh.



Jiya started her acting journey in 2013 with a Telugu movie and in 2015, she made her television debut in the series Love by Chance. She is now confirmed to appear in this season and with her strong social media fan following, she is selected to spend the day in Bigg Boss house with other contestants.
