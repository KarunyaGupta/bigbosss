AKANKSHA PURI

Indian Actress/Model

Akanksha Puri is a known Indian actress and model who is all set to participate in Bigg Boss OTT Season 2. She became famous for her role as Nandita Menon in the Hindi movie Calendar Girls which came out in 2015. Akanksha has shown her talent by working in Hindi TV shows and films from South India.



Akanksha grew up in Indore Madhya Pradesh and had a regular middle-class lifestyle. She attended a village school for her primary education. Sports played a significant role in her life and she actively took part in tennis and basketball competitions during her school and college years. People lovingly called her monkey because of her playful and lively nature. Akanksha has made a name for herself in the entertainment industry, particularly for her role of Goddess Parvati in the series Vighnaharta Ganesh.
