MANISHA RANI

Social Media Influencer

Manisha Rani, an Indian dancer and social media influencer, is recognised for her entertaining content posted across online platforms. She earned popularity through her engaging TikTok videos. Before achieving recognition, Manisha worked as a waitress and background dancer at weddings. Her hard work paid off as she successfully established her presence in the entertainment industry.



Manisha Rani harboured dreams of participating in the reality show ‘Dance India Dance’ in 12th grade, but her father objected. In an interview, she talked about this and revealed her dreams of becoming an actor and dancer, and her failed attempts to convince her father to allow her to move to Kolkata with a friend to pursue these ambitions. Despite her father’s refusal, Manisha left a letter for her father and moved to Kolkata to follow her dreams after which her father didn’t speak to her for a year.
