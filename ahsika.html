AHSIKA BHAITA

Reeler Creater

Aashika Bhatia who is a famous reeler and content creator joined the Bigg Boss OTT 2 house as a surprise contestant. They gave hints about their entry on social media. Aashika wrote a message on Instagram suggesting that something big was happening in her life and that she would be away from social media for a while. The show’s host Salman Khan has extended the program for two more weeks which got mixed reactions and the new finale date is now August 13.



When Aashika entered the house of Bigg Boss OTT 2 as a contestant, she started conversations and revealed some past controversies with Elvish. Elvish was surprised when he heard Aashika’s voice before seeing her face. They playfully teased each other and it was mentioned that Elvish had made a video in the past where he made fun of Aashika’s appearance. Aashika clarified that they didn’t know each other when the video was made but there were some issues and Elvish apologize for that. But Aashika made it clear that she don’t have any issue with him.
